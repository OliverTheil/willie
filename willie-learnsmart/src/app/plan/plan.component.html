First of all:
The complete plan works with the Pomodoro technique. But willie, what is pomodoro?
It's a time management from Francesco Cirillo (1980) and stands for tomatoe. That's right! It's a tomatoe technique. Ok, no it isn't. It gets its name from a timer that breaks down tasks into smaller 25min-pieces. After a 25 minute task you get a 5 minute break. Also very common are tasks with a duration of 50 minutes and 10 minute breaks.
In a nutshell, after every little task of 25 or 50 minutes you get a break.

But within that time period, here's what you need to do:

- full concentration
- no smartphone! Airplane mode and put it away
- stay hydrated
- get snacks like some apple pieces or cookies
- listen to something relaxing like lofi-beats
